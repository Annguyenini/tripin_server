# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 4, 2026*

## Information We Collect
- Account information (name, email) when you sign up
- Your profile image(Your self or any that you decide to use)
- Photos and media you upload within the app
- Location data when you pin memories to a place
- Basic usage data to improve the app


## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve the TRIPPING experience
- To store and display your trips and memories
- To enable trip sharing with others you choose

## We Do Not
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your information without your consent
- Use your data for advertising

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely on our servers. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
